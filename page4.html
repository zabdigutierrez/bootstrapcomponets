3DS

The Nintendo 3DS is transportable game console developed by Nintendo. The Nintendo 3DS was released in the year two thousand and eleven. A few months after the release of the 3DS in Japan it was released worldwide. One of the features of the 3DS is that it can show stereoscopic 3D effects without the need of wearing 3D glasses while using the game console. The Nintendo 3DS also has features like tag modes. Two popular tag modes are the Street Pass and the Spot Pass. The 3DS uses its 3D cameras and the virtual console to let the player download the games and play them like they could on older video game systems. When the Nintendo 3DS first launched the rates of sales were not that great. Nintendo did many efforts to change the outcome of the selling rates. Nintendo decided to reduce the price of the 3DS by eighty dollars. Even with the reduction price of the 3DS people were not buying the 3DS. Nintendo announced that with buying the 3DS at the price it was when it first released, Nintendo would give you free FamiCom games and ten free Game Boy Advance games from the Nintendo eShop. People could not refuse the offer that Nintendo had given so the plan to get the sales rating of the Nintendo 3DS up worked. The plan worked well and allowed the Nintendo 3DS to become a successful product of Nintendo. The Nintendo 3DS became Nintendo's most successful transportable video game consoles in the first two of release to the public. The Nintendo 3DS was developed in new ways multiple times.
